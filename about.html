About Me

My name is Nicole Young. I am orginally from New York and moved to Melbourne in October 2018. I tried to reflect my journey on the main page showing an image of Sorrento Beach in Victoria, overlayed with an image of New York City. I started my career in Banking at the Financial Times located on Wall Street. Then I moved through big banking institutions like Bank of America and Deutsche Bank as a financial analyst. I have worked at a Hedge Fund, then at a small boutique investment firm that closed. My last stop in New York was working as an engineering intern for 5 months at Grasshopper Bank which was a start-up digital bank.
I have been transitioning to a new country and into parenthood since I have arrived in Australia. I am now ready to continue on my path to become a web developer. Since moving to Australia, I have been eager to get back to coding. The Coder Academy has been a great experience and I can't wait to put what I've learned to practice.
