Marketplace App called Flossy

The purpose of my marketplace is to facilitate the buying and selling of lightly used fashion items like clothing, shoes, accessories and children’s wear. People who are into fashion tend to buy many items but only wear them a handful of times. Sometimes it could just be once if it’s a special occasion. With Flossy, a user will be able to buy or sell lightly used fashion items from other users of the app.

https://flossyapp789.herokuapp.com/
